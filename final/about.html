What we do
We are an organization who strives in helping students find the solution to their academic problems. FAQ
Do you need to be registered in order to browse tutors?

No, you do not have to be registered or logged in at all. This webiste provides the users with the ability to browse tutors around the Western Cape anonymously. You will only be required to register and log in if you wish to advertise yourself as a tutor.

Does the posting of a tutor require any banking information?

No, it does not. As explained in the terms and conditions, we do not spport in app purhases. We only display information such as cellphone numbers and email addresses. Both the tutors and users will be responible for sharing these financial information amongst each other, external from this website.

Are users allowed to post multiple adverts using the same account?

Yes, as long as they are using the same account. If the same person is using 2 different accounts, the accounts will be removed. So, just make sure that the multiple advertisements belongs to the same account.

Is there a way for users to display all tutors within the northen suburbs?

No, not exactly. We provide all suburbs within the Western Cape to choose from, such as Durbanville, Bellville, and Kenrdige. We do not provide combined suburbs as an parameter to choose from, you will have to search tutors in each suburb individually.

How do we know if the tutors are legit?

We, the Western Cape Tutors, make use of email validation and verification to ensure that a user actually exists. We also have the ability remove accounts that has been reported as "being fake accounts". We can't guarantee that all users are legit, but we will remove as much possible accounts reported to us.
